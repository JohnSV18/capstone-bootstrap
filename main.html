Learn about us

Promoting growth and development through competitive soccer in Queens, New York. Eastern Queens F.C. was founded in 2016 by Jonathan and Jimmy Puma. They grew up playing travel soccer throughout their childhood, but had to travel over an hour out of Queens just to be able to compete at the highest level. Soon they realized, they aren’t the only people from Queens who were forced to travel outside of the borough in order to play competitive and organized soccer. With so much passion and talent produced in Queens, it was time for a club to reflect that determination and dedication. Eastern Queens F.C. aims to bring that same level of elite competition to the people of Queens.

Prepare your children for their brightest future. Eastern NYC Football Club is innovating the field of youth soccer player development through our emphasis on discipline, hard work and determination that is instilled in each player at every training session, which then translates to their daily lives. We offer a unique training experience, designed to enhance individual skill, collective identity, and personal growth, which we believe is the key to participatory engagement. We welcome all prospective players to participate in our training sessions.
